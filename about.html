Company Overview

Founded in 2017, C&Y Material Limited began as an OEM manufacturing partner for internationally recognized brands.

Today, we have evolved into a trusted global manufacturer specializing in innovative audio products, charging accessories, and customized private-label solutions — spanning research and development, manufacturing, branding, packaging, and worldwide logistics support.

With advanced production facilities, strict quality control systems, and continuous innovation, we create products designed for real-world scenarios — from better sleep experiences to immersive gaming and everyday connectivity.

Our Vision

To become one of the world's most trusted and innovative manufacturers of audio and smart electronic products by delivering exceptional quality, advanced technology, and sustainable business partnerships.

Our Mission

Create innovative products that improve everyday life, provide world-class OEM and ODM manufacturing, and build long-term partnerships based on trust and mutual growth.
