Who We Are

Innovation. Quality. For Every Life.

Founded in 2017, C&Y Material Limited began as an OEM manufacturing partner for internationally recognized brands.

Today, we have evolved into a trusted global manufacturer specializing in innovative audio products, charging accessories, and customized private-label solutions — spanning research and development, manufacturing, branding, packaging, and worldwide logistics support. With advanced production facilities, strict quality control systems, and continuous innovation, we create products designed for real-world scenarios — from better sleep experiences to immersive gaming and everyday connectivity.
